Engineering Innovation

Harnessing the power of the Archimedes Screw Turbine.

01. Material

High-density reinforced polymers (HDPE) to prevent corrosion and ensure a lifespan of over 25 years in water.

02. Design

A low-head Archimedes screw design that operates efficiently even with minimal water pressure or slow flow rates.

03. Impact

100% Fish-friendly architecture that maintains the local aquatic ecosystem while generating clean electricity.

SYSTEM EFFICIENCY: 85%

How it Works

The AquaVolt turbine utilizes the gravitational potential energy of flowing water. As water enters the screw, it rotates the blades, which are connected to a high-efficiency generator.

Optimized for Egypt

Designed specifically for the irrigation canals and drainage networks found in Egypt's agricultural sectors.
